About

I'm an instructional designer who came to the field through the classroom. After years of teaching, I learned that the difference between content and learning is design — and I've spent every project since refining that craft.

My work blends pedagogical rigor with modern eLearning tooling. I build courses in iSpring Suite, but more importantly I do the upstream work that makes those courses effective: audience analysis, learning objectives mapped to Bloom's taxonomy, storyboards that anticipate cognitive load, and assessments that actually measure transfer.

I'm currently open to instructional design and eLearning developer roles, on-site or remote.

  • Design ADDIE, SAM, Backward Design
  • Assessment Formative & summative, rubric design
  • Media Storyboarding, scriptwriting, screen capture
