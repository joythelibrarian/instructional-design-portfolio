About

Hayward, California

For more than fifteen years, I've stood in front of classrooms — community colleges, four-year universities, high schools, adult learners — mostly here in the Bay Area.

Eight of those years were in higher education, where I learned that what I loved most wasn't the lecturing. It was the design. The hours spent shaping a lesson so it actually landed. The moment a student leaned forward because something clicked. That quiet love for the craft of designing learning is what brought me to instructional design.

I'm currently completing my internship with the IDOL Courses Academy, where I'll earn my CPTP (Certified Professional in Training and Performance) certification in summer 2026. I've worked through every theoretical stage of course creation using both ADDIE and SAM frameworks, and partnered closely with a subject matter expert to build a full course from analysis to evaluation.

I build interactive eLearning modules in iSpring Suite, Articulate 360, and PowerPoint; create custom animations in Vyond; produce and edit audio in GarageBand and Audacity; and record screen-based tutorials in Camtasia. I integrate generative AI thoughtfully into my workflow — both to accelerate design and to teach the digital literacy skills learners actually need.

I'm currently open to instructional design and eLearning roles — full-time, contract, or remote.

Beat the Bot:
Building Your AI Career Agent

A task-centered eLearning workshop that helps IDOL Academy students close the "employability gap" by translating teacher-talk into corporate language. Learners build a custom AI Career Agent — a persistent chatbot trained to act as their personal recruiter, copywriter, and interview coach — and use it to produce ATS-optimized resumes and practice behavioral interviews.

The course reframes the opaque hiring process as a "Firewall" the learner is engineered to penetrate, transforming job-search anxiety into a gamified technical challenge.

Audience
IDOL Academy students
Format
Self-paced eLearning workshop
My Role
Instructional Designer
Frameworks
ADDIE & SAM, with SME collaboration

Terminal Learning Objective

Given access to a Generative AI tool and a target job listing, learners will construct a custom AI Job Search Bot to produce tailored professional assets that achieve an ATS compatibility score of 80% or higher, and successfully simulate a behavioral interview response using the STAR method.
